The Future of Gaming

Enabling Tokens

A token in a game allows the player to improve his functionality and purchase virtual goods or services. Game developers can use this technique to create powerful game mechanics, thus allowing them to monetize their games via the blockchain.

Enabling NFTs

With NFTs, our goal is to allow easy access and develop new technologies to guarantee an extensive amount of NFTs at low cost, even for games that are not suitable for this technology. Certain characters or items might only be available in limited supply or to certain players. The most common use of tokens in games is as digital collectibles.

Allowing Trading

Trading is a crucial component of game ecosystem. It allows gamers to earn money by selling or renting their items on the market, and developers to earn money by using this mechanism to implement monetization models. We believe that NFTs trading will create a larger and more engaged player base.
